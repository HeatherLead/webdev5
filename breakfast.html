Cupcake

Description

These delightful cupcakes are soft, fluffy, and topped with creamy frosting. Perfect for any occasion, these sweet treats are a crowd-pleaser with their moist texture and rich flavor.

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 1 hr 31 mins
  • Additional: 15 mins
  • Total: 1 hr 66 mins
  • Servings: 8
  • Yield: 8 servings
Nutrition Info

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Frosting of choice
