Pie

Description

Orange Pie is a delightful and zesty dessert that brings together the tangy sweetness of oranges with a buttery, flaky pie crust. This citrus-infused treat offers a refreshing twist on classic pies, perfect for brightening up any occasion with its vibrant flavor and cheerful appearance.

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 1 hr 31 mins
  • Additional: 15 mins
  • Total: 1 hr 66 mins
  • Servings: 8
  • Yield: 8 servings
Nutrition Info

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made or homemade pie crust
  • 4 large oranges (zested and juiced)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Whipped cream or powdered sugar (optional, for garnish)
